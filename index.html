Privacy Policy

Aman Enterprises - Fresh Groceries Delivered

Last Updated: February 9, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Aman Enterprises ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or place an order, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Account Credentials: Password (encrypted)
  • Delivery Information: Address, location coordinates
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (we do not store complete card numbers)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data and preferences
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Create and manage your account
  • Send order updates and notifications
  • Improve our app and services
  • Provide customer support
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Delivery Partners: To fulfill your orders
  • Payment Processors: To process transactions securely
  • Service Providers: Who help us operate our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure payment processing through certified gateways
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our app may use:

  • Local storage to save your preferences
  • Analytics tools to improve user experience
  • Firebase services for authentication and notifications

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google (Sign-In, Maps, Firebase)
  • Payment gateways (Cashfree, Razorpay)
  • Cloud services for data storage

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion request, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
